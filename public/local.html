Source policy

Copyright-safe and citation-first

We use public-domain, open-license, permission-safe content, or metadata links to source catalogs. Copyrighted books are not hosted without permission.

AI summaries are not original sources and must be labeled: “AI-enhanced summary based on listed sources.” Every image must show source, creator where known, license and attribution.
